    Which of the following is not a characteristic of a corporation?
    • A) Right of succession
    • B) Created by operation of law
    • C) Unlimited liability of shareholders
    • D) Artificial being

    Explanation: Corporate Characteristics

    Correct Answer: C

    A corporation provides limited liability to its shareholders, meaning they are only liable up to the amount of their investment. Unlimited liability is a characteristic of a sole proprietorship or a general partnership.

    A corporation issues 10,000 shares of P100 par value ordinary shares for P150 per share. What is the amount to be credited to the Share Premium account?

    Explanation: Share Capital Issuance

    Correct Answer: 500,000

    The total cash received is P1,500,000 (10,000 shares * P150). The ordinary share capital account is credited for the par value: P1,000,000 (10,000 shares * P100). The excess over par is credited to share premium: P1,500,000 - P1,000,000 = P500,000.

    When a corporation acquires its own shares, how is this transaction recorded under the cost method?
    • A) As an asset
    • B) As a deduction from total shareholders' equity
    • C) As a deduction from retained earnings only
    • D) As an increase in share premium

    Explanation: Treasury Shares

    Correct Answer: B

    Treasury shares are not assets. Under the cost method, the cost of the acquired shares is recorded in a contra-equity account, which reduces the total shareholders' equity on the statement of financial position.
